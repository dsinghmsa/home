Welcome!

This is a custom Chrome profile made by Devin. It should log out your accounts when you close the browser, but make sure to log out just in case! It's pre-configured to do these things:

  • Block ads and annoyances
  • Prevent pages from tracking you (without breaking them)
  • Speed up page loading and responsiveness
  • Don't interfere with any other profiles

Please don't change any settings as they aren't meant to be changed. Here's some useful quick access links:

  • Google Search
  • Google Classroom
  • Gmail

Why?

Many websites (including online games) have tons of advertising and tracking built into them, so that's what this stops.

If any pages require you to turn off your ad blocker, simply go to the extensions button and disable UBlock Origin for that site.
